# ThoughtStack (Fridge Combo #72)

**ThoughtStack** is a minimalist micro-journaling tool designed to capture and retrieve your fleeting thoughts, book reviews, movie impressions, and sudden inspirations with zero friction. It turns your browser into a persistent, searchable stack of memories.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Focuses entirely on "stacking" and "finding" thoughts.
- **Micro-Journaling**: Encourages short-form entries (approx. 10 lines) to lower the mental barrier to writing.
- **Zero Friction**: Search while you think. Find while you write.

## Key Features
- **Dual-Mode UI**: 
    - **Add Mode**: A clean interface for capturing new thoughts with title and content.
    - **Search Mode**: Focuses entirely on your archives. When you click the search bar, the input area disappears to maximize visibility on mobile screens.
- **Smart Retrieval**: Supports AND search (multi-word) to find specific entries instantly.
- **Relative Time**: Displays timestamps like "3h ago" or "Just now" for immediate context of your recent activities.
- **Privacy First**: No login, no tracking, no ads. All data is stored locally in your browser's `LocalStorage`.
- **Management & Safety**: 
    - **Edit Mode**: Hide destructive actions (Delete) behind a toggle to prevent accidental data loss.
    - **Backup**: Export your entire stack as a JSON file for safe-keeping.
- **Quick Share**: One-click "Copy" button to grab your note with its title and date, formatted for sharing.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff
- **Main Text/UI**: #333
- **Border Radius**: 15px (Strictly applied to all elements)
- **Mobile Optimized**: Prevents UI shifting and keyboard interference through dynamic layout adjustments.

## Privacy Policy
- No login required.
- No tracking or analytics.
- No advertisements.
- Browser-only storage (Your data never leaves your device).
